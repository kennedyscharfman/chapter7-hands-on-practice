Located in the Heart of Sedona

At Casita Sedona Bed & Breakfast you will be on a glorious, relaxing vacation. Whether you are visiting for hiking, sightseeing, or just relaxing, you will find the beauty of Sedona inspiring.

Luxurious Rooms

Stay in one of our beautifully appointed rooms with private bath, fine linens, and scenic views of the red rocks.

Hearty Breakfast

Each morning enjoy a hearty breakfast that includes your choice of eggs, freshly baked pastries, fruit, and beverages.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts are long-time Sedona residents who know the area well and enjoy sharing their favorite places with guests.

Guided Hikes

Explore the breathtaking scenery of Sedona with a guided hike. Our experienced guides will take you on unforgettable adventures through red rock canyons and desert landscapes.
